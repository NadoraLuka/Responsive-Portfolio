About Nader Mashy

I am Nader Mashy, I currently live in Knoxville, TN but originally, I am from South Sudan. In 2001, my family moved from Sudan to Cairo, Egypt to pursue a to move USA. The process of coming to USA, kept us in Egypt for 6 years. Life was risky in Egypt, but my parents understood that sometimes, one must risk it to get the biscuit.. Finally, in 2007 my family moved to USA, where I thought life would be a cake walk based on the stories I have heard and what I have seen in social media. Oh, my word! Just imagine someone who does not speaks English at all, get thrown straight into high school and their class is biology. That “someone” is me. Now, I have my Associates degree in electrical engineering from Pellissippi State Community college and, I am a structural engineer designer. Currently, I am a student at Vanderbilt University’s Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. The lesson I have learned from life, you got to risk it to get the biscuit.
